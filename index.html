howdowework.com will be a interesting website that will help employees to give feedback for different companies.

Welcome to howdowework.com

Soon... howdowework.com will change the way we work !

Mission

The objective of this website is to ensure the business collaboration satisfaction. An employee will be able , after he ended up a contract or a collaboration with a company, to leave some feedback to the company. This way, a new employee that wants to find a new job , will be able to have a clear overview of that the public opinions are.

Benefits for the employee

The employee who wants to hire or establish a contract with a company, can easily search howdowework.com for feedback (Before going to an interview or being involved into a recruitment process).

An employee who works/worked with the company for which he/she wants to leave a feedback and/or a story must have an account on howdowework.com .

An identity verification of the account is optional, and the user will receive additional points/badges which we'll be able to supply in the future.

A person can say anything he/she wants about the company but only if he has an account on howdowework.com.

Eg : An employee that used to work for a company, knows what he's talking about rather than a friend of an angry employee is not eligible to speak in his name. That's why we require a registered user.

However, the account data will remain confidential between the employee and howdowework.com , so any user can reserve the right to remain anonymous on a public feedback.

Any other people that will want to add some stories about the company will have a different section for that. Eg : Sofia didn't work at Cegeka, but her boyfriend did. She has a special section where she is able to post a story like . " A great company, my bf was really happy about ... etc... "

Employer benefits

The website objective is to help the employees more than companies, but the company is also able to add feedback to a person. ( Of course, feedback to your profile will be visible just with your approval ).
